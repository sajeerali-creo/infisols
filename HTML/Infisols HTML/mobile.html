Mobile App Development

Our Mobile App Development Services

We strongly believe that with mobile apps, development services should cover more than just the coding. Our mobile app developers work with you through mobile strategy, mobile app design, development, app quality assurance, and delivery. We also offer specific enterprise apps development services that can be customized to suit the needs of your business, including hiring mobile app developers for staff augmentation; long-term team integration; and full-service app development. Below is a breakdown of the mobile application development services we offer.

Android Apps Development Services

Much like iOS, we have been providing Android mobile app development services since day 1, working with clients that include PayPal, Enercare, Shell, and more. With a team of dedicated Android app developers, designers, architects and more, we offer Android mobile app development services that cover smartphones, tablets, smart TVs, wearables, and other devices supporting Android operating systems.

Enterprise Mobile Apps Development Services

For enterprise mobile apps, development services need to be flexible and adaptable to the needs of the organization. Enterprises may require full development, staff augmentation, or even team integration. Our enterprise mobile app development services are designed to provide the flexibility your organization needs to get products to market, whether it be integrating our mobile app developers with your team; having us work on a specific facet of your mobile product or strategy; or providing our end-to-end mobile application development services from concept to delivery.

Mobile App Design Services

When creating mobile apps, development services need to be integrated with strong UX/UI design. Products are fundamentally created to be used by people; if the design of the app is poor, it won’t be successful. At Clearbridge, our mobile application development services are integrated with strategy and mobile app design to ensure all facets of your product have been planned and created with your end users in mind. This includes concepting and ideation, wireframes and user flows, UI and UX design and final polish.

Mobile App Strategy

Mobile app strategy is a central component of our mobile app development services mix, encompassing product definition, feature planning, roadmapping and more. Our mobile app developers, product strategists, architects and designers work together with you to identify challenges and goals, conduct market research, and create a mobile strategy aimed at long term, sustainable success.

Mobile App Testing & Maintenance

Mobile app development services aren’t complete without proper quality assurance testing. Products that go to market with bugs provide a poor user experience and immediately turn off users, resulting in high abandonment rates and bad reviews. Clearbridge Mobile offers full quality assurance services on multiple devices and in multiple environments.
